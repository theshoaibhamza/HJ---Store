Shipping Policy

Fast, secure, and reliable shipping worldwide with transparent pricing.

Last updated: December 26, 2025

Effective date: December 26, 2025

Our Shipping Promise

We're committed to delivering your beautiful jewelry safely and promptly. Every order is carefully packaged and tracked to ensure your precious pieces arrive in perfect condition.

Free Australian Shipping: Enjoy complimentary standard shipping on all orders over $100 AUD within Australia. No minimum order requirements for other destinations.

Australian Domestic Shipping

Standard Shipping

Cost: FREE on orders over $100, otherwise $9.95

Delivery: 2-7 business days

Tracking: Provided via Australia Post

Express Shipping

Cost: $19.95

Delivery: 1-3 business days

Tracking: Real-time tracking included

Same Day (Metro Areas)

Cost: $29.95

Delivery: Same day (order by 2 PM)

Available: Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane CBDs

Australian Shipping Coverage

  • Major Cities: Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide, Darwin, Hobart
  • Regional Areas: Available to all Australia Post serviceable locations
  • Remote Areas: Extended delivery times may apply (5-10 business days)
  • PO Box Delivery: Available for all domestic orders

Packaging & Protection

Your jewelry deserves the best protection during transit. We take special care in packaging each order.

Our Packaging Process

  • Individual Pouches: Each piece placed in soft velvet jewelry pouches
  • Luxury Boxes: Items presented in elegant Chandni Jewellery branded boxes
  • Protective Padding: Bubble wrap and cushioning materials for extra protection
  • Secure Sealing: Tamper-evident packaging to ensure product integrity
  • Discreet Shipping: No external indication of valuable contents

Special Handling

  • Fragile Items: Extra padding and "Fragile" labels applied
  • High-Value Orders: Additional security measures and signature confirmation
  • Gift Orders: Beautiful gift wrapping available upon request
  • Multiple Items: Individually wrapped to prevent scratching

Tracking & Delivery

Order Tracking

  • Tracking Number: Provided in dispatch email for all orders
  • Real-time Updates: Track your order's journey from dispatch to delivery
  • SMS Alerts: Optional delivery notifications available
  • Delivery Attempts: Carrier will attempt delivery 2-3 times

Delivery Options

Home/Office Delivery

Direct delivery to your specified address during business hours. Signature may be required for high-value items.

Safe Drop

Secure delivery to a safe location at your property when you're not available (domestic orders only).

Post Office Collection

Collect from your local post office if delivery attempts are unsuccessful. Notification card left.

Delivery Delays: While rare, delays may occur due to weather, customs processing, or courier issues. We'll keep you informed of any significant delays.

Special Circumstances

Holiday Shipping

  • Christmas Period: Order by December 18th for Christmas delivery
  • Valentine's Day: Order by February 10th for Valentine's delivery
  • Mother's Day: Order by May 1st for Mother's Day delivery
  • Extended Processing: Allow extra processing time during peak seasons

Address Changes

  • Before Dispatch: Contact us immediately to update delivery address
  • After Dispatch: Contact the courier directly using your tracking number
  • Incorrect Address: Customer responsible for re-delivery fees
  • Undeliverable: Orders returned to us will incur re-shipping charges

Lost or Damaged Packages

  • Insurance Coverage: All orders automatically insured for full value
  • Damage Claims: Report within 48 hours of delivery with photos
  • Lost Packages: We'll investigate and arrange replacement or refund
  • Theft: Report to local police and provide crime reference number

Customs & Import Duties

International customers should be aware of potential customs charges and import regulations.

Customer Responsibilities

  • Customs Duties: All duties, taxes, and fees are customer's responsibility
  • Accurate Information: Provide correct recipient details and contact information
  • Customs Clearance: Assist with any customs queries if required
  • Refused Packages: Customer liable for return shipping and customs fees

Customs Documentation

  • Commercial Invoice: Detailed description and value of items
  • Accurate Declarations: All items declared at full retail value
  • Country of Origin: Clearly marked on all documentation
  • Harmonized Codes: Correct classification for jewelry items
Important: Chandni Jewellery cannot be held responsible for packages held up in customs or any associated fees. Customers are advised to check their country's import regulations.
