About Chandni Jewellery

Bringing you authentic Pakistani and Indian jewellery with traditional craftsmanship and modern elegance. Based in Brisbane, Australia.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for traditional South Asian jewellery, Chandni Jewellery has been serving the Brisbane community and customers worldwide with authentic, handcrafted pieces that celebrate cultural heritage and timeless beauty.

Our carefully curated collection features traditional Pakistani and Indian designs, from everyday elegance to bridal grandeur. Each piece is selected for its quality, authenticity, and the story it tells.

We believe that jewellery is more than an accessory – it's a connection to culture, tradition, and personal expression. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or adding to your everyday collection, we're here to help you find the perfect piece.
