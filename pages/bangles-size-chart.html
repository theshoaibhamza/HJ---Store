Bangles Size Chart & Fitting Guide

Find your perfect bangle size with our comprehensive sizing guide and measuring instructions.

How to Measure Your Wrist

Finding the perfect bangle size is essential for comfort and style. Follow our simple guide to determine your ideal bangle size.

Step-by-Step Measuring Guide

  1. Use a measuring tape:

    Wrap a flexible measuring tape around your wrist just below the wrist bone where you would normally wear a bracelet.

  2. Alternative method - String:

    If you don't have a measuring tape, use a piece of string or paper strip. Wrap it around your wrist and mark where it overlaps, then measure against a ruler.

  3. Add comfort allowance:

    Add 0.5 to 1 inch to your wrist measurement for a comfortable fit that allows the bangle to slide over your hand.

Perfect Fit Tips

Tight Fit

Bangles should slide over your hand with minimal effort. If it's too difficult to put on, try the next size up.

Loose Fit

A bangle that slides around too much may feel uncomfortable. Consider sizing down for a more secure fit.

Perfect Fit

The ideal bangle should slide over your hand comfortably and sit securely on your wrist without being too tight.

Important Notes

  • Bangle sizes may vary slightly between different styles and materials
  • Consider the width of the bangle - wider bangles may require a slightly larger size
  • If you're between sizes, we recommend choosing the larger size for comfort
  • Contact our customer service if you need assistance with sizing
