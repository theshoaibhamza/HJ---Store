Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. Learn about our refund and return process.

Last updated: December 26, 2025

Effective date: December 26, 2025

Our Commitment to You

At Chandni Jewellery, we stand behind the quality of our products and are committed to ensuring your complete satisfaction with every purchase. If you're not entirely happy with your jewelry, we're here to make it right.

Return Policy

Return Timeframe

  • 14 days: You have 14 calendar days from the date you received your item to request a return
  • Christmas Returns: Items purchased between December 1st and December 24th may be returned until January 15th
  • Notification Required: You must notify us within the return timeframe to initiate the return process

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • Item must be in its original condition and unworn
  • All original packaging, tags, and documentation must be included
  • Items must not show signs of wear, damage, or alteration
  • Custom or personalized items may not be eligible for return (contact us to discuss)
  • Gift cards and promotional items are non-returnable

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons:

  • Earrings (due to health and hygiene regulations)
  • Custom-made or personalized jewelry
  • Items damaged by misuse or normal wear
  • Items returned without original packaging

Refund Process

1

Contact Us

Email us at info@chandnijewellery.com.au with your order number and reason for return. We'll provide you with a return authorization and instructions.

2

Package Your Return

Carefully package your item in its original packaging. Include all accessories, documentation, and the return authorization form.

3

Ship Your Return

Send your package to our returns address using a trackable shipping method. You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item was defective or we made an error.

4

Processing & Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-10 business days to your original payment method.

Exchanges

We currently offer exchanges on a case-by-case basis. If you would like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or style, please contact us to discuss your options. Exchange availability depends on stock and the specific item.

Exchange Process

  • Contact us within 14 days of receiving your order
  • Original item must meet return eligibility criteria
  • Desired exchange item must be available in stock
  • Price differences may apply and will be charged or refunded accordingly
  • Customer pays return shipping; we cover shipping for the replacement item

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, we sincerely apologize and will make it right immediately.

Quick Resolution: Contact us immediately with photos of the damaged item. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund, including return shipping costs.

What to Do

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of receiving the damaged item
  2. Provide photos showing the damage or defect
  3. Include your order number and description of the issue
  4. Keep the item and packaging until we provide instructions

Refund Methods & Timeframes

Credit/Debit Cards

Refunds will appear on your statement within 5-10 business days after processing. The exact timeframe depends on your bank or card issuer.

PayPal

Refunds to PayPal accounts are typically processed within 3-5 business days.

Bank Transfer

Direct bank transfers may take 2-7 business days depending on your financial institution.

Note: Refund processing times may be extended during peak holiday seasons or due to banking delays beyond our control.

International Returns

We accept returns from international customers with the following considerations:

  • Customer is responsible for all return shipping costs and customs fees
  • Items must be declared accurately for customs purposes
  • Return timeframe may be extended to account for international shipping delays
  • Refunds exclude original shipping costs and any customs/duties paid
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping method with insurance

Store Credit Option

As an alternative to refunds, we offer store credit with additional benefits:

  • Store credit never expires
  • Can be used for any future purchase
  • May be combined with promotional offers
  • Faster processing than refunds (1-2 business days)
  • Can be transferred to gift cards for gifting

To request store credit instead of a refund, simply mention this preference when contacting us about your return.

Questions About Returns?

Our customer service team is here to help make your return process as smooth as possible.

Email: info@chandnijewellery.com.au

Response Time: Within 24-48 hours

Include: Order number, reason for return, and any relevant photos

Contact Customer Service

Policy Updates

We may update this refund policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last modified" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our return and refund procedures.

For significant policy changes, we will notify customers via email or through notices on our website.
